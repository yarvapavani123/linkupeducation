Principle Job

The Principal leads the school, overseeing staff, curriculum, and operations to ensure a safe, inclusive, and high-quality learning environment. They manage budgets, foster community relations, and drive academic and institutional success.

Qualifications

  • A Master's degree in Education or a related field.
  • Prior teaching and administrative experience.
  • Strong leadership, communication, and organizational skills.
  • Manage school operations, including budgets, schedules, and facilities.

English Teacher

The English Teacher creates engaging lessons to develop students' language and critical thinking skills. They evaluate progress, provide feedback, and inspire a love for literature. A supportive and dynamic learning environment is prioritized.

Skills Required

  • Strong verbal and written skills to teach language concepts clearly.
  • Ability to maintain an organized, engaging, and disciplined environment.
  • Crafting innovative lessons to inspire interest in language and literature.
  • Evaluating student progress and providing constructive guidance.

Social Teacher

The Social Studies Teacher teaches history, geography, economics, and civics, designing lessons that help students understand society and cultures. They foster critical thinking, encourage discussion, and prepare students to be informed citizens.

Skills Required:

  • Deep understanding of history, geography, economics, and civics.
  • Ability to develop students' analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Clear presentation of complex concepts and active classroom discussion.
  • Using diverse teaching methods to keep students motivated & involved in learning.

Maths Teacher

The Math Teacher teaches algebra, geometry, calculus, and statistics, designing lessons that develop problem-solving and critical thinking skills. They encourage analytical thinking and foster a positive attitude toward mathematics.

Skills Required:

  • Strong knowledge of various mathematical topics and techniques.
  • Ability to guide students in solving complex mathematical problems.
  • Encouraging students to think critically and logically.
  • Ability to explain complex mathematical concepts clearly and engagingly.

Biology Teacher

The Biology Teacher teaches biological concepts like anatomy, genetics, and ecology, creating engaging lessons that promote understanding. They encourage scientific inquiry and foster a passion for life sciences through experiments and discussions.

Skills Required:

  • In-depth knowledge of biological sciences and principles.
  • Ability to conduct experiments that demonstrate biological concepts.
  • Guiding students to think analytically and explore biological phenomena.
  • Clear presentation of complex biological concepts in an engaging way.

Physics teacher

The Physics Teacher teaches principles of physics, including mechanics, electricity, and thermodynamics. They design lessons that promote understanding through experiments and problem-solving, fostering curiosity and a passion for science.

Skills Required:

  • Deep understanding of physics concepts and theories.
  • Ability to conduct experiments that demonstrate key physics principles.
  • Strong skills in guiding students through complex physics problems.
  • Clear explanation of abstract concepts and engaging classroom discussions.

Hindi teacher

The Hindi Teacher teaches reading, writing, speaking, and comprehension skills, creating engaging lessons on grammar, literature, and culture. They encourage participation and foster a love for the language. They build predictive models and extract insights for business decision-making.

Skills Required:

  • Strong command over Hindi grammar, vocabulary, and literature.
  • Ability to convey complex concepts clearly in Hindi.
  • Creative lesson plans that inspire interest in the language and culture.
  • Knowledge of Hindi literature, history, and traditions to enrich lessons.
